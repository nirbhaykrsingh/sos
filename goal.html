Who We Are

Sanaya Gangal is a current student at Booker T. Washington High School for the Performing and Visual Arts. During her studies of the art of classical music and her role of enhancing her vocal technique skills in her high school career, Sanaya Gangal works with underprivileged children through her own non-profit organization called Songbirds of Service. With her passion for music and love for singing, she is able to spread her wings to teach kids music and guide them to be one with the art. In addition to inspiring children in the form of musicianship, she also studies Hindustani (Indian) classical music, thereby truly believing that music has no boundaries.
