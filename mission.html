Mission Statement

Songbirds of Service will recruit volunteers to go into the community at large to work with underprivileged children to foster a passion for the arts in order to build a strong, positive sense of identity in each child through creative and unique self expression. In addition, Songbirds of Service will perform fundraisers to raise money in order to improve the quality of life of children in Bal Anand Orphanage in Mumbai, India.

What we do?

  • We use our musical and artistic talents to raise money for children in need.
  • We furnish children with knowledge of art and music and give them an opportunity to develop a love for the arts.
  • We provide children with an opportunity to learn and experience the cultures of art and music.
  • We empower young children to appreciate different forms of art.
  • We inspire the learning of the arts and the development of advanced knowledge regarding what art brings to the community.
  • We enhance the quality of education by introducing forms of art that can inspire young children to initiate in creative aspects in their life.
