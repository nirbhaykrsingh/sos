Motivation

Why India? Its my roots. The idea of Songbirds of Service came to my mind after I visited the orphanage I came from in Mumbai, India called Bal Anand.

Since then, I have wanted to do something to better the lives of the kids there. The one thing that I know that I’m able to share with them is my joy for music and art. Initially I was just going to create this organization for the orphaned kids but then I realized that I could do so much more with this by helping underprivileged kids here in Dallas as well.

"The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others."
―Mahatma Gandhi
